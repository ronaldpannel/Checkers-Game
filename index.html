Checkers Game

Game Rules

  1. Black always first to play
  2. Players take alternate turns
  3. Game is played on the brown squares only
  4. Only forward diagonal left and right moves are allowed
  5. Unless capturing an opposing checker by jumping over it, player can only move one square each go
  6. If a player has an opportunity to capture an opposing checker, this move must be made
  7. If the player has multiple capture opportunities he can only pick one to play
  8. Multiple checkers can be captured if the opposing checkers are positioned on alternate squares allowing multiple jumps
  9. If a checker reaches the first row of the opponents side of the board, the checker becomes a King and diagonally in all directions
  10. If a player captures all his opponents checker that player wins, If the board becomes locked where no moves can be made the game is a draw
